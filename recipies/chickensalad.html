Chicken Salad

Description

Chicken salad is a versatile and timeless dish that combines cooked chicken with a variety of ingredients to create a flavorful and satisfying meal. Typically, it features chopped or shredded chicken mixed with a creamy binder such as mayonnaise, sour cream, or yogurt, and is often enhanced with additions like celery, onions, herbs, and sometimes fruits or nuts. This mixture can be served in various ways, including as a sandwich filling, atop a bed of greens, or accompanied by crackers. The adaptability of chicken salad allows for numerous variations, catering to diverse tastes and dietary preferences. Its enduring popularity stems from its simplicity, ease of preparation, and the comfort it offers, making it a staple in many households and a favorite for picnics, lunches, and light dinners.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup blanched slivered almonds
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 cups chopped, cooked chicken meat
  • 1 rib celery, chopped

Steps to Make

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Place almonds in a frying pan. Toast over medium-high heat, shaking frequently. Watch carefully, as they burn easily.
  3. Mix together mayonnaise, lemon juice, and pepper in a medium bowl.
  4. Toss with chicken, toasted almonds, and celery.
  5. Enjoy!
